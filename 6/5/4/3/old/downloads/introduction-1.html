SIX CLASSES OF FOOD

They are six classic of food. These inludes:

  1. Carbohydrates
  2. Protein
  3. Fats and oil (Lipids)
  4. Vitamin
  5. Mineral salt
  6. Water

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are foods the gives the body energy.
Examples includes

  • Rice
  • Bread
  • Yam
  • etc.

Protein

Protein are foods that support the body building mechanism.
Examples includes

  • Beans
  • Egg
  • etc.

Fats and Oil

Fats and oil are also knows as Lipids and they are importants energy stores in the body.
Examples are:

  • Butter
  • Beef fat
  • Fish oil
  • etc.
